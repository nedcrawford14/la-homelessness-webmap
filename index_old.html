Homelessness in Los Angeles

A web mapping analysis of homelessness rates, rent burden, and 311 encampment call density in the City of Los Angeles.

Project Overview

This map explores the spatial relationship between homelessness, housing pressure, and public reports of encampments.

Layers

  • Rent burden
  • Homeless persons per 1,000
  • 311 encampment call density

Methods

Data were processed in ArcGIS Pro and published to ArcGIS Online for use in a custom web application.
